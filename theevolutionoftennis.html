Clothes Throughout Time

Racquets and Their Changes

The Tennis Balls Changed Too

Clothes that tennis players wear today are different from what they used to wear back in the day. Men wore long white pants with white sweaters. Women wore long white skirts and white shirts. Now, women wear shorter skirts and dresses sometimes shorts. Men wear shorts and muscle tees. Clothes today are odor resistant and gets rid of sweat once absorbed into the clothing.

Racquets have changed if you didn't know that. When tennis was first played, the palms were used to hit the ball over the net. Eventually, they made wooden racquets with strings and long handles. Today, tennis racquets have a strong tension which helps the player take control of the ball and have stability. Racquets today are different colors and types.

Tennis balls changed too for your information. The first tennis ball was made out of leather. They were stuffed with hair or wool. They were very hard to hit with their palms. Eventually, we developed new tennis balls. These tennis balls are fuzzy on the outside and have rubber on the inside. These new balls are easier to control during a match. They also bouncce which is fun, right?
