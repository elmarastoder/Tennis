Tennis

"I play each point like my life depends on it."

-Rafael Nadal



Tennis is a sport in which you need a court, a racquet, a net, some tennis balls, and don't forget your opponent! Tennis is all over the television these days. Tennis is a sport played year round. Tennis can be played indoors. There are different tennis tournaments played year- round. There are only four grand slam tournaments known as Wimbledon, US Open, French Open, and the Australian Open. Tennis has certain rules which need to be followed in order to win the point. The most amount of sets that can be played is 5 sets. It is timed to see how long it has been played for. As you can tell, tennis is a very interesting sport with a history and legends.
